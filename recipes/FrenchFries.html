French Fries

Description

Thinly cut potatoes deep fried in vegetable oil, topped with cheese and scallions

Ingredients

    Potatoes
    Oil
    Salt
    Pepper

Instructions

  • Thinly cut potates
  • Place the thinly cut potatoes into cold water for 15-30 mins
  • Heat up the oil in a deep pan
  • Cook the potatoes in the pan until golden brown
  • Once done, take them out and pat down the oil with a paper towel
  • Season the fries
